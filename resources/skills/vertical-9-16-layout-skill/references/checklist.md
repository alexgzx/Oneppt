# Vertical 9:16 Structural Self-check

## P0 - Not Deliverable

- Content exceeds 900x1600 or is clipped.
- Primary text-bearing modules use absolute/fixed positioning instead of grid/flex flow.
- Body text is below 18px, headings below 24px, or auxiliary text below 12px.
- The page has no top-to-bottom reading path.
- A chart/list/table is unreadable at phone-screen width.

## P1 - Should Fix

- The screen has no single memorable message.
- The middle zone does not carry the main value.
- The bottom zone is decorative only when the page needs a takeaway or support.
- Too many same-weight cards create a long list instead of a designed story.
- A chart lacks a nearby interpretation sentence.
- A two-column pocket is too narrow for its text.

## P2 - Consider Optimizing

- Support facts can be grouped under fewer labels.
- The hook could be stronger as a question, number, or concrete claim.
- The sequence rhythm is uneven across rows.
- Source/footer text could be simplified into one auxiliary line.
