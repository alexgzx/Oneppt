# Vertical 3:4 Structural Self-check

## P0 - Not Deliverable

- Content exceeds 1200x1600 or is clipped.
- Text-bearing modules use absolute/fixed positioning instead of grid/flex flow.
- Body text is below 18px, headings below 24px, or auxiliary text below 12px.
- The poster has no strong visual or conceptual anchor.
- Chart/table/list content is unreadable.

## P1 - Should Fix

- The page is a long stack of equal cards.
- Bottom area has no synthesis, implication, source, or final support.
- Small facts are not grouped under meaningful labels.
- A two-column pocket is too narrow or too dominant.
- A metric/data card lacks a nearby interpretation.
- Steps or evidence groups do not use the middle/lower canvas.

## P2 - Consider Optimizing

- The anchor could be larger or placed more decisively.
- Evidence bands could be grouped into fewer sections.
- A chart could become a hero metric or rank list.
- Source/footer text could be simplified into one auxiliary line.
