# Square 1:1 Layout Catalog

This catalog describes 1200x1200 square-card structures. It is structural only: style controls color, type mood, decoration, shadows, and texture.

## Zone Skeletons

### `center-anchor`

- **Use for**: most square cards.
- **Zone sketch**: focal object near center, support arranged around or below it.
- **Balance rule**: the center must be visually dominant and corners must feel intentional.
- **Failure sign**: the card becomes a set of equal modules with no anchor.

### `top-center-bottom`

- **Use for**: title + visual/data + takeaway.
- **Zone sketch**: compact top title, large center object, concise bottom band.
- **Balance rule**: center carries the card; top and bottom frame it.
- **Failure sign**: the top area holds all content while the center is passive.

### `quadrant-grid`

- **Use for**: four categories, framework cells, or paired comparisons.
- **Zone sketch**: 2x2 grid with equal or intentionally weighted quadrants.
- **Balance rule**: cells stay parallel unless one quadrant is deliberately emphasized.
- **Failure sign**: cells become long prose boxes.

## Patterns

### `center-hero-orbit`

- **Input shape**: one central concept, metric, or claim with 3-5 facets.
- **Structure recipe**: center block plus surrounding chips/cards in a stable grid.
- **Budget rule**: center is largest; support stays short.
- **Failure signs**: support appears before the center; orbit items collide or wrap heavily.

### `top-center-bottom`

- **Input shape**: title, one main visual/data object, and a conclusion/source.
- **Structure recipe**: top title zone, central visual zone, bottom takeaway band.
- **Budget rule**: central zone is the largest; top and bottom remain compact.
- **Failure signs**: center is only decorative; bottom band repeats the title.

### `quadrant-card`

- **Input shape**: four categories, 2x2 framework, or four comparable takeaways.
- **Structure recipe**: 2x2 grid with one emphasized quadrant when hierarchy matters.
- **Budget rule**: each cell has a short title and 1-2 lines.
- **Failure signs**: cells are not comparable; each cell contains paragraph copy.

### `claim-evidence-band`

- **Input shape**: big claim/quote plus 2-4 proof points.
- **Structure recipe**: large claim in upper or center zone; compact evidence band below or around it.
- **Budget rule**: evidence supports the claim, not competes with it.
- **Failure signs**: all proof points are equal-weight heroes; claim text is too small.

### `square-data-card`

- **Input shape**: one hero metric, compact chart, or ranked list.
- **Structure recipe**: hero number/chart, interpretation sentence, optional 1-2 metric chips.
- **Budget rule**: data object is sized for square reading and has nearby interpretation.
- **Failure signs**: chart labels are too wide; interpretation is far from data.

### `stacked-pair`

- **Input shape**: before/after, problem/solution, option A/B.
- **Structure recipe**: two stacked zones or two side-by-side zones with generous gutters.
- **Budget rule**: use matching fields for fair comparison.
- **Failure signs**: one side has more fields; side-by-side text becomes narrow.
